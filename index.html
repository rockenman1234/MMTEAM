MelodyMixer Team #34

We're Team #34, a dynamic group of five Georgia Tech students collaborating on an exciting second project as part of GT-2340. Our team consists of four Computer Science majors and one Computer Engineering major, bringing together a diverse range of skills in software development, system architecture, and problem-solving.


Our websites are built using TailWindCSS.com to provide an interactive, user-friendly, and fast experience! We’ve integrated the Spotify API directly to offer a detailed and output while avoiding issues occuring from recent changes in Spotify's API. We also used Figma to design a basic website, and Trello+Teams for asynchronous communication and collaboration.
